I'm originally from the East Coast (Queens, NY), and I moved to the Bay Area to teach high school science as a Teach For America corps member.

I create imaginatively and obsessively, and love to draw inspiration from both current trends (user interface and technologies) and older influences (aesthetics).

My original background is in education and the natural sciences. The former provides me with insight about how to build intuitive interfaces that speak to the needs of all users, and the latter helps me fix bugs and make critical implementation decisions in an analytial manner.
