I'm originally from the East Coast, and I moved to the Bay Area to teach high school science as a Teach For America corps member.

I create imaginatively and obsessively, and love to combine current trends (user interface and technologies) with some older aesthetic influences. My design aesthetic skews towards minimalism and simplicity.

My original background is in education and the natural sciences. The former provides me with insight about how to build intuitive interfaces that speak to the needs of all users, and the latter inspires me to fix bugs and make critical implementation decisions in an analytical manner.
